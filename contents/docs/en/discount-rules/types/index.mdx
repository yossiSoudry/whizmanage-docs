## Discount Rule Types

The Whizmanage system supports 7 different discount types, each tailored for a different purpose. Understanding the differences between types will help you choose the right tool for each promotion.

### Overview of All Types

<Step>
<StepItem title="Product Adjustment">

**Definition:** Direct discount on specific products

**When to use:**
- Discount on a specific category
- Promotion on a specific brand
- Discount by product attributes
- General sale on product group

**Examples:**
- "20% off all electronics"
- "$50 off shoes"
- "30% off summer clothing"

<img src="https://placehold.co/800x300?text=Product+Adjustment" alt="Product Adjustment" />
</StepItem>

<StepItem title="Cart Adjustment">

**Definition:** Discount on entire cart amount

**When to use:**
- General discount on entire purchase
- "Over amount X get discount" promotion
- Automatic coupon for all customers
- Discount based on cart amount

**Examples:**
- "10% off entire cart"
- "$100 off purchases over $500"
- "15% off entire cart for Hanukkah"

<img src="https://placehold.co/800x300?text=Cart+Adjustment" alt="Cart Adjustment" />
</StepItem>

<StepItem title="Bulk Discount">

**Definition:** Tiered discounts based on purchase quantity

**When to use:**
- Encourage bulk purchases
- Wholesale discounts
- "The more you buy, the more you save" promotions
- Quantity-based discount tiers

**Examples:**
- "Buy 3 items - 10% off, Buy 5 - 20% off"
- "1-2 units: regular price, 3-5 units: 15% off"

<img src="https://placehold.co/800x300?text=Bulk+Discount" alt="Bulk Discount" />
</StepItem>

<StepItem title="BOGO (Buy One Get One)">

**Definition:** Buy X Get Y **from the same product** free/discounted

**When to use:**
- "Buy 2 Get 1 Free"
- Promotions on single product
- Encourage purchasing quantities of same item
- Clearance promotions

**Examples:**
- "Buy 2 shirts, get 1 free"
- "Buy 3 books, get 1 at 50% off"
- "Buy 1 Get 1 Free"

<img src="https://placehold.co/800x300?text=BOGO" alt="BOGO" />

<Note title="Important" type="warning">
BOGO works only on the same product. If you want to buy product X and get a different product Y, use BXGY.
</Note>
</StepItem>

<StepItem title="BXGY (Buy X Get Y)">

**Definition:** Buy product(s) X and get **different** product(s) Y free/discounted

**When to use:**
- Cross-selling - selling complementary products
- "Buy expensive product get cheap one free"
- Bundle promotions
- Encourage purchasing additional products

**Examples:**
- "Buy laptop get mouse free"
- "Buy 2 shirts get pants at 50% off"
- "Buy shampoo get conditioner free"

<img src="https://placehold.co/800x300?text=BXGY" alt="BXGY" />
</StepItem>

<StepItem title="Shipping Discount">

**Definition:** Discount on shipping cost

**When to use:**
- Free shipping over amount
- Discount on shipping for entire cart
- Discounted shipping for specific products
- Shipping promotions

**Examples:**
- "Free shipping over $200"
- "50% off shipping"
- "Free shipping on electronics"

<img src="https://placehold.co/800x300?text=Shipping+Discount" alt="Shipping Discount" />
</StepItem>

<StepItem title="Spend Bundle">

**Definition:** Bundle deals - Spend X get discount

**When to use:**
- "Spend $500 get $100 off"
- "Bonus" promotions based on spending
- Encourage cart size increase
- Loyalty promotions

**Examples:**
- "Spend $300 get $50 off"
- "Spend $1000 get $200 gift"

<img src="https://placehold.co/800x300?text=Spend+Bundle" alt="Spend Bundle" />
</StepItem>
</Step>

## Quick Comparison Table

| Type | Main Purpose | Complexity | Best For |
|------|--------------|------------|----------|
| **Product Adjustment** | Discount on products | ⭐ | Simple promotions |
| **Cart Adjustment** | Discount on cart | ⭐ | General coupons |
| **Bulk Discount** | Tiered discounts | ⭐⭐ | Large quantities |
| **BOGO** | Buy X Get Y (same product) | ⭐⭐ | BOGO promotions |
| **BXGY** | Buy X Get Y (different product) | ⭐⭐⭐ | Cross-selling |
| **Shipping** | Shipping discount | ⭐ | Free shipping |
| **Spend Bundle** | Spend X Get Y | ⭐⭐ | Loyalty promotions |

## Summary

Each discount type is designed for a different purpose. Choose the appropriate type for your promotion and enjoy easy and efficient management!
